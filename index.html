About Me

Kevin Marquez

I am currently a full-time student, work a part time job, and run my own small business. I work as a bartender during the week and help my father out with his landscaping and irrigation business on the weekends. During covid I started an online business, and it opened my eyes to the potential in the computer world. At the time I was a Mechanical Engineering Major but getting involved with retail arbitrage and then moving on to the crypto space to earn money intrigued me into learning how to efficiently manage a business. I am now a Computer Information System major looking forward to where this degree will lead my future. What I love most about this major is that you can never know enough, there is always room to grow.My free time consist of either working out or spending time with my lovely girlfriend along with our two dogs.

Personal Goals

My short-term goal is to succeed in my university career until I graduate in about three more semesters. I am committed to giving all my classes my best effort to pass every one of them. The goal is to leave college with background information that will differentiate myself from others in the work field. I also want to continue to scale my online business to the point where it will become my main source of income for the time being. My goal post graduation is to be employed by a medium to large business that appreciates my skills and work ethic. It would be ideal to be able to purchase my own house within a year of graduation to enjoy a home with my family. The end goal is to reach financial freedom as quickly as possible, and I will do everything I can to excel during my university years as it is an important stepping stone.
